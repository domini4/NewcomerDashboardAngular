How to use this dashboard:

  1. Below listed is the detailed view. Press the down arrow on the right side of each class to view all open comments.
  2. What does each status mean?
    • Open  - Newcomer comment with no replies after the comment. The issue is still open.
    • Replied by you - Newcomer comment with replies from you after the comment. The issue is still open.
    • Replied by other  - Newcomer comment with replies from others, but not you after the comment. The issue is still open.
